The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask was released in 2000 for the Nintendo 64 and 2003 for the Gamecube. The game is set in an alternate reality called Termina doomed to be destroyed by the moon in 3 days. The player is tasked to save Termina from the Skull Kid who took possesion of a powerful artifacts called Majora's Mask.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild was released on 2017 for the Nintendo Switch. The game is set in an apocalyptic Hyrule, with Link waking up from a 100 year slumber with no memories. The player is tasked with saving Zelda from Calamity Ganon. This game is the first to utilize an open-air world, very much unlike its predecessors.

1,234 views

The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time was released on 1998 as the first 3D Zelda game. The game is set in Hyrule, with Link tasked to defeat the dark king Ganondorf. Link uses the power to time travel to gain the aid of sages to seal away Ganondorf.
